❓ FAQ

Got
questions?

Everything you want to know about Funngro, answered clearly.

Who can join Funngro? +
Any Indian student between 13–21 years old can join Funngro for free. No prior experience or qualifications needed. Just a smartphone and the will to hustle.
How do I get paid? +
All payments are made via UPI directly to your bank account. Once a task is approved (usually within 24 hours), your earnings are instantly transferred. No delays, no gift cards — real rupees.
Is Funngro safe? +
Absolutely. Every brand on Funngro is verified. We have parental consent features for users under 18. We never share personal data with brands without explicit permission.
How much can I earn? +
Earnings vary by task type and effort. Simple surveys pay ₹50–₹200. Creative tasks like video editing or content writing can earn ₹300–₹1,000+ per task. Top Teenlancers earn ₹10,000+ per month.
Do I need any special skills? +
Not at all! Many tasks like surveys and product testing require zero skills. As you grow on the platform, you can unlock higher-paying creative tasks. Funngro grows with you.
